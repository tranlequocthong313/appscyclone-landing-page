Introduce

This FRONTEND MASTER PROGRAMMING course is suitable for those of you who are IT students or those who want to become a comprehensive Front-end Dev to work in companies. After the course, you will have completed 5 projects like working and have full knowledge from basic to advanced of a Front-end Dev. The Frontend Master course is divided into 3 main phases:

- Frontend Newbie: 6 week duration (2 sessions/week). Class time 18h45 - 21h45 Tuesday and Saturday.

- Web Responsive: 6 week duration (2 sessions/week). Class time 18h45 - 21h45 Tuesday and Saturday

- ReactJS Master: 6 weeks duration (3 sessions/week). Study time 18h45 - 21h45 on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays
